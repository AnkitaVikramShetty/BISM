Internationals from all cultural & religious backgrounds are welcome to our events

Conversational English Classes

Informal classes arranged during weekdays for internationals to learn and practice conversational English and adapt to American culture. Native English-speaking volunteers will facilitate conversations on topics related to everyday life and English usage.

Weekend Conversation Class

Weekend English Conversation Class will begin on September at the Evangelical Community Church. The event will take place on Saturdays. Transportation provided. Contact Randy Hawes at rahawes@indiana.edu for more information. For more details go to www.iubism.org/eng_conv.htm.

Day time Conversation Class

Day time English Conversation Class for international women will begin on September at Sherwood Oaks Christian Church. The event will take place from 9:30 to 11:00am each Tuesday morning. Transportation and childcare provided. Contact Rani Rajamani at rani@rajamani.org for more information. For more details go to www.i-connections.org/iwec/.

Book Club for Women

International Book Connection (IBC) is a book club for international women to read and discuss a book together with a group of American and international women. One unique feature of IBC is to use the book club to help international women improve their English. At IBC you will also have opportunities to discuss topics of general interest for women. International Book Connection for women will begin on September at Sherwood Oaks Christian Church. The event will take place from 9:30 to 11:00am each Wednesday morning. Transportation and childcare provided. Contact Rani Rajamani at rani@rajamani.org for more information. For more details go to www.i-connections.org/ibc/ .

Social Events/Sightseeing Trips

Regular local and out-of-town activities that will allow internationals to develop friendships and enjoy American culture and attractions. Past events have included picnics, apple-picking, sporting events, day trips, holiday parties, snow skiing, movie nights, and sightseeing trips to Chicago, Cincinnati, St. Louis, and Florida.

Apple Picking Trip (TBA)

Come spend the afternoon tasting a variety of apples for free and purchase your choice of apples, pumpkins, and other fall fruits and vegetables. Also, enjoy the fall festival and craft fair held right at the orchard.

Friendship Partners

Internationals will be matched with American families and individuals so that mutually enjoyable friendships can develop. Students may be invited by their American friends to visit their homes, attend family functions, and observe holidays over the course of the year. Contact Ed Huff at huffed@indiana.edu or 812-327-5549 for more information.

Bible Study

For Christians

Weekly Bible Studies for Christian international students, scholars and families takes place on various weekday evenings. This is an opportunity to study the Bible with other internationals and American friends in a culturally relevant manner. Contact Elan Rajamani at rani@rajamani.org | 812-325-9047 or Ed Huff at huffed@indiana.edu | 812-327-5549 for more information.

Bible Discussions

For all internationals (Group and Individual)

Weekend and weekday group and individual Bible discussions for international students, scholars and families from all backgrounds take place throughout the week. This is an opportunity to learn more about the Bible and the Christian faith no matter what religion you come from.
